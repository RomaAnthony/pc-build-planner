# Knowledge Base

This folder is for deep part research, not current prices.

Use it after market capture to explain why parts are good, bad, acceptable, or only fallback choices.

## Difference From Market Data

- `01_Market_Data/` stores raw captures from shops and comparison sites.
- `02_PC_Builds/01_Price_Refresh/` stores current price decisions and old-vs-new price logs.
- `05_Knowledge_Base/` stores durable knowledge: spec tiers, compatibility, tradeoffs, and why a part is worth choosing.

## Planned Guides

- `RAM/RAM_DECISION_GUIDE.md` - DDR5-6000 CL30 target, fallback timings, 2-stick vs 4-stick AM5 notes.
- `GPU/GPU_DECISION_GUIDE.md` - RTX 5080 choices now; RTX 5070 Ti and RTX 4080 Super later.
- `Motherboard/MOTHERBOARD_DECISION_GUIDE.md` - B850 vs X870/X870E and which features matter.
- `SSD/SSD_DECISION_GUIDE.md` - PCIe 4 vs PCIe 5, real-world value, thermals.
- `PSU/PSU_DECISION_GUIDE.md` - ATX 3.1, 12V-2x6, 850W vs 1000W.
- `Case/CASE_DECISION_GUIDE.md` - Lancool 207 Digital and compact airflow options.
- `Cooling/COOLING_DECISION_GUIDE.md` - 360 AIO choices and airflow setup.

## Rule

Do not mix price scraping into these files. Prices belong in dated market snapshots. Knowledge files should answer:

- What specs matter?
- What tradeoffs are acceptable?
- What should be filtered out before focused price searches?
- Which 3-5 products deserve exact store checks?
