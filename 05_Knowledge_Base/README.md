# Knowledge Base

This folder is for deep part research, not current prices.

Use it after market capture to explain why parts are good, bad, acceptable, or only fallback choices.

## Current Reality Check

The active build/order truth is in `00_Hub/Current_Order/`, not in this folder.

Some knowledge files were written earlier around MSI B850 Tomahawk and Lian Li Lancool 207 assumptions. Keep them as research history and comparison notes, but do not treat those older target names as the current order. The current ordered/pending build uses Gigabyte X870E AORUS Elite WiFi7 and NZXT H7 Flow RGB unless `00_Hub/Current_Order/` says otherwise.

## Difference From Market Data

- `01_Market_Data/` stores raw captures from shops and comparison sites.
- `00_Hub/Current_Order/` stores real purchases, receipts, delivery details, and remaining buy decisions.
- `02_PC_Builds/01_Price_Refresh/` stores price decisions and old-vs-new price logs.
- `05_Knowledge_Base/` stores durable knowledge: spec tiers, compatibility, tradeoffs, and why a part is worth choosing.

## Rule

Do not mix price scraping into these files. Prices belong in dated market snapshots. Knowledge files should answer:

- What specs matter?
- What tradeoffs are acceptable?
- What should be filtered out before focused price searches?
- Which 3-5 products deserve exact store checks?
