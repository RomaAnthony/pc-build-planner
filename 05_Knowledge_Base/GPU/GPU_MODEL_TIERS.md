# GPU Model Tiers — RTX 5080 Family

For: Ryzen 9 9900X · 1440p 165Hz · RTX 5080 target

---

## Bottom Line

**Within RTX 5080, the difference between models is cooler quality and noise, not performance.** MSI Ventus 3X OC is the best value. MSI Gaming Trio is the best mid-range. Suprim / ROG Strix are 15-25% more expensive for 1-2°C cooler temps and marginally quieter fans.

For this build: the Ventus 3X OC at 4,899 PLN (Poland) or ~499k HUF (Hungary) is the right pick. The savings go into the platform.

## Why Ventus 3X OC Is The Pick

- 303mm — fits Lancool 207 with tons of room
- 2-slot — leaves breathing room for bottom intake
- Dual BIOS (silent/performance) — silent mode is fine for most
- 36-month warranty from MSI official sellers (x-kom Poland)
- Real price: ~407k HUF equivalent in Poland vs ~499k HUF in Hungary

**The saving vs Hungary (~92k HUF) is the biggest single-part saving in the entire build.**

## Power Connector Note

All RTX 5080 models use the **12V-2x6 (12VHPWR)** connector. The be quiet! Pure Power 13 M 1000W has a native 12V-2x6 cable — no adapter needed. The RM1000x ATX 3.1 also has native support.

This matters: the early 12VHPWR melting issues were largely caused by users not fully seating the adapter. A native PSU cable is longer, less bulky, and safer.
