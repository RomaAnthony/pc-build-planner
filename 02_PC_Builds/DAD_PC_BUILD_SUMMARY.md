- coding and dashboards

Example projects:
- personal finance and investment database

The RTX 5080 can come later. The first purchase is the platform: CPU, RAM, SSD, motherboard, PSU, cooler, and case.

The MacBook gives portability, battery, screen, and Apple ecosystem. The desktop gives more work-machine power per forint and can be upgraded later.

## Final Logic

The plan is not to buy everything at once.

Best path:
1. Buy the strong PC platform first.
2. Use integrated graphics temporarily.
3. Buy the RTX 5080 later only if the price, seller, and warranty are good.

This keeps the first purchase controlled while keeping the future path open.
