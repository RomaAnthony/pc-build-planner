# PSU Working Shortlist

Updated: 2026-06-13

## Current Recommendation

For this build, stay with a **1000W ATX 3.1 / PCIe 5.1 PSU with native 12V-2x6**.

Reason:
- Future RTX 5080.
- Ryzen 9 9900X target.
- Large glass airflow case.
- Long-term stability matters more than saving a few thousand HUF on the PSU.

850W can work for many RTX 5080 builds, but it is not the best direction here unless budget gets tight or a very trusted 850W model is much cheaper.

## Avoid / Be Careful

- **MSI MPG A1000G PCIE5 1000W (306-7ZP7C11-CE0)**: avoid for now. The imported research pack flagged serious test-failure risk. Do not mix this with **MSI MAG A1000GL**.
- **EVOLVEO G1000 PCIe 5.0 1000W** at 39,095 HUF: too cheap/risky for a build with RTX 5080 later.
- **LC-Power / Kolink / very cheap unknown 1000W units**: not worth the risk here.
- **White/SFX/SFX-L units**: only if the case/build plan changes. Current build direction is black ATX glass airflow.
- **Older ATX 3.0 / 12VHPWR-only models**: not automatically bad, but prefer native ATX 3.1 / PCIe 5.1 / 12V-2x6 now.

## Decision Logic

Current value choice:
- **be quiet! Pure Power 13 M 1000W (BP028EU)** if seller is trusted and final product page confirms ATX 3.1 / PCIe 5.1 / native 12V-2x6.

Current safest choice:
- **Seasonic Focus GX-1000 (2024) / Focus GX-1000 v4** if it stays near 60,000 HUF.

Strong alternative:
- **Corsair RM1000x ATX 3.1** if it gets close to Seasonic price or has a clearly better seller/warranty situation.

Budget fallback:
- **Corsair RM1000e 2025** if the price gap matters and seller is clean.

Poland:
- Not clearly better for PSU right now.
- Corsair RM1000x at 647 PLN is good, but after conversion/logistics it is close to Hungarian options.
- Seasonic GX-1000 at 687 PLN is worse than the Hungarian 59,990 HUF capture.
- Buy from Poland only if bundling with other parts and the seller is strong.

## Next Checks Before Buying

For the final candidate page, verify:
- exact model number
- ATX 3.1 support
- PCIe 5.1 / native 12V-2x6 cable
- warranty length
- seller rating and return policy
- cable length and case clearance
- no model-name mixup

Short version: the wide search is good enough. Next PSU step should be seller-page checking for **be quiet! Pure Power 13 M 1000W**, **Seasonic Focus GX-1000 2024**, and **Corsair RM1000x ATX 3.1**.
