## Decision Logic

Roman is not gaming-first, so do not overpay for X3D unless the price gap is small.

Best fit:

- Ryzen 9 9900X: 12 cores / 24 threads, strong productivity, good enough gaming, integrated graphics, sensible power/heat.
- Ryzen 7 9700X: still the value fallback if platform budget needs to stay low.
- Ryzen 9 9950X: real productivity upgrade over 9900X, but only if the price gap is acceptable and cooling/budget are comfortable.

Treat carefully:

- Ryzen 9 9900X3D: attractive balanced chip, but the current price gap is too large for a mostly non-gaming build.
- Ryzen 7 9800X3D: excellent gaming CPU, but not the best fit for Roman's database/productivity priority.
- Ryzen 9 9950X3D: technically great, financially hard to justify right now.
- Tray/OEM CPUs: only if warranty route and seller are clearly good.

## Current Conclusion

The Ryzen 9 9900X is currently the clean target CPU for this build.
