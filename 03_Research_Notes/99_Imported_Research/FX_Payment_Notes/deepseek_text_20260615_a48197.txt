- Higher `R` → stronger HUF (you need fewer HUF to buy the same amount of UAH).
- Lower `R` → weaker HUF.

---

## 3. How to Show the Purchase in Foreign Currency

When recording the transaction, show both:
1. The original foreign currency amount – `P_UAH`
2. The converted home currency amount at the **current rate** – `P_HUF`

```text
Purchase amount: 10,000 UAH
Exchange rate (HUF→UAH): 1.25
HUF equivalent: 8,000 HUF
